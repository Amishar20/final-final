Tech Departments

Chassis

Our team specializes in designing and developing vehicle chassis, the foundational framework that provides strength, rigidity, and enhanced crash resistance.

Powertrain

Our powertrain team specializes in developing high-performance systems that efficiently transfer power from the engine to the wheels.

Steering & Suspension

Our steering and suspension team is dedicated to designing systems that ensure precise handling, maintain wheel traction, and effectively absorb shocks.

Brakes & Tyres

Our brakes and tires team specializes in the design, testing, and maintenance of high-performance braking systems.

Electrical

Our electrical team specializes in wiring, connections, and component adjustments for optimal system performance and battery management.

Electronics

Our electronics team focuses on electronic component integration, configuration, and battery system optimization

Computers

Our computer team specializes in the integration of advanced control systems and powertrain optimization.

Team Oorja

From solar car to electric Go-Kart , Team Oorja has designed and fabricated multiple projects which competed in national level competition that brought laurels to the University.
