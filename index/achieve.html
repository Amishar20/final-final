IKC 2023

Venturing into the dynamic and rapidly evolving realm of electric vehicles, the team showcased their adaptability by achieving an impressive overall 3rd place in the EV category, a testament to their groundbreaking advancements and innovations. Furthermore, they garnered recognition for the Best Design Presentation, showcasing their ability to combine aesthetics and functionality seamlessly in their debut at the esteemed E-go-kart competition, solidifying their position in the realm of electric go-kart racing.

GKDC 2019

In 2018-2019 the team scaled and dominated the G.K.D.C- 2018 held at Buddh international Circuit (Greater Noida) with complete authority with over 192 teams participating from the starting day.
Overall Runner Up
Best Autocross
Best Driver
Best Business plan
Apart from these trophies, we also gained good positions in other events that didn’t involve a trophy or a prize but did play role while accumulating the marks for the final rankings.
Cost report – 1st Position
Endurance race – 3rd Position
Engineering design – 2nd Position
Acceleration – 2nd Position overall, 1st in the final lineup
Disassembly – 3rd prize.

GKDC 2018

2017-2018 the team designed and fabricated a Go-kart for G.K.D.C- 2018 held at Kari Motor Speedway (Coimbatore) where teams participated from all over the country. The team performed well with being placed at an all India rank of 27th out of over 190 teams and the fabricated vehicle had the 2 nd best acceleration in lineup.

2016

The team started its venture with the EFFI Cars electric competition (2016) where engineering students from all over India fabricate their own electric vehicles. The team designed and fabricated their own solar electric passenger vehicle and won the Best Design Award
