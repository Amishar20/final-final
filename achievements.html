General information

  • Competition: IKC
  • Year: 2023

Indian Karting Competition

Venturing into the dynamic and rapidly evolving realm of electric vehicles, the team showcased their adaptability by achieving an impressive overall 3rd place in the EV category, a testament to their groundbreaking advancements and innovations. Furthermore, they garnered recognition for the Best Design Presentation, showcasing their ability to combine aesthetics and functionality seamlessly in their debut at the esteemed E-go-kart competition, solidifying their position in the realm of electric go-kart racing.

General information

  • Competition: GKDC
  • Year: 2019

Go Kart Designing Competition

In 2018-2019, our team showcased exceptional prowess at the G.K.D.C-2018, held at the Buddh International Circuit (Greater Noida), dominating with authority among 192 participating teams. Securing the Overall Runner-Up title, along with Best Autocross, Best Driver, and Best Business Plan awards, highlighted our comprehensive excellence. Beyond trophies, our achievements extended to a 1st position in the Cost Report, 3rd in the Endurance Race, 2nd in Engineering Design, and impressive 2nd overall and 1st in the final lineup for Acceleration. Additionally, we secured a 3rd prize in Disassembly, underscoring our team's all-encompassing success and competitive edge.

General information

  • Competition: GKDC
  • Year: 2018

Go Kart Designing Competition

In 2017-2018 the team designed and fabricated a Go-kart for G.K.D.C- 2018 held at Kari Motor Speedway (Coimbatore) where teams participated from all over the country. The team performed well with being placed at an all India rank of 27th out of over 190 teams and the fabricated vehicle had the 2 nd best acceleration in lineup.

General information

  • Competition: EFFI
  • Year: 2016

EFFI

The team started its venture with the EFFI Cars electric competition (2016) where engineering students from all over India fabricate their own electric vehicles. The team designed and fabricated their own solar electric passenger vehicle and won the Best Design Award.

Team Oorja

From solar car to electric Go-Kart , Team Oorja has designed and fabricated multiple projects which competed in national level competition that brought laurels to the University.
